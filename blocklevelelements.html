MICRONOVA INDUSTRIES is an ISO 9001 certified manufacturing company based in Jamnagar (India). Our roots are planted back in 1990 by MARAKANA FAMILY MEMBERS with a small manufacturing unit. Since then we are carrying forward this legacy as a professional manufacturer in the development & manufacturing of Precision Metal Components. Listening closely is key to us – and so we can offer our business partners exactly what they need. To us, metal is more than material. Backed by our in-depth understanding of various grades of COPPER and BRASS the manufacture of semi-finished & finished products, we can also provide solutions to the growing requirements for material properties and miniaturization, never failing to convince our customers - in this now already for 33 years. With a variety of product line & providing every micro details in Precision way, we aim to provide our customer’s solution of their ALL NEED UNDER ONE ROOF. With the facility of in-house manufacturing including from BRASS ROD TO BRASS FINISHED PRODUCTS we try our best to provide the best precision solutions. Our product and service portfolio provides answers and solutions to all those challenges that these megatrends are bringing with them: electrical equipment, plumbing, automobile, engineering, aerospace, medical devices, fasteners & fittings, refrigerating technologies, electromobility, connectivity. Even today, nothing in our ultra-modern, high-tech world works without copper alloys.
